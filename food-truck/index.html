About Us


In the Fall of 2019, the Hospitality Management and Culinary Arts Department purchased a Food Truck to enhance the learning experience for the students enrolled in our hands-on learning labs.  The food truck will be used to spotlight student chefs who are working towards obtaining their AAS in Hospitality or Culinary Arts.  The truck will also serve as a catering vehicle for off-site functions served by the student ran Hospitality and Culinary Arts Club.   The program will also be expanding our offerings of hybrid and web-blended classes where students can be enrolled online at various Nebraska locations and will test out of certain competencies via the catering truck that will be used as a mobile cooking lab.  The faculty and staff also have plans to attend career fairs and expos with the truck allowing potential recruits to join in the action of a live cooking demonstration at their location.  The food truck is an exciting addition to our program.
